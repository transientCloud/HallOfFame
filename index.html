SoCC Hall of Fame  |   Top-50 Authors   |   Top-10 Papers

ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing (SoCC) is the premier venue for cloud computing research. Started in 2010, SoCC marks its 10th anniversary this year. During this period, a total of 328 papers have been published by 1180 unique authors. This hall of fame recognizes the most prolific authors as well as highly cited papers. Explore more!

This list is inspired by similar efforts on ISCA, MICRO, ASPLOS, SOSP, and FAST. We gather the publication data from the ACM digital library, and author information from csrankings.org. We plan to add more trends and insights about SoCC in the coming days. Direct all queries to Supreeth Shastri who maintains this repository.
